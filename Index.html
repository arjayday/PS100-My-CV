About Me!

A layout artist, whose love for design is matched only by my dedication to constant improvement. A student by day, meticulously crafting layouts that spoke volumes with their simplicity and elegance. In the bustling institution where I studied, deadlines often seemed to chase everyone like shadows. Yet, amidst the chaos, I found solace in my work. My room became a sanctuary adorned with sketches, color palettes, and posters of my past works.

Philosophy

"aut inveniam viam aut faciam." or "I shall either find a way or make one." This speaks true to me, as a person, and as a layout artist, since I always have to adapt and create new ways or techniques in order to either break or apply new trends everyday.

To sum up, I always try to be:

  • Adaptable
  • Resilient
  • Consistent
  • Creative (slight lhungs)
